Tutorials for the BSc course Probability and Statistical Inference for the academic year 2025/26.

Description
======

This course introduces the fundamental concepts of probability and statistical inference, providing students with the tools to model and analyze uncertainty in real-world situations. Topics include probability distributions, random variables, expectation and variance, parameter estimation, hypothesis testing, ANOVA, Multiple Linear Regression, confidence intervals and prediction intervals. The course emphasizes the application of these concepts to real data and includes practical exercises and numerical examples using R. Students will develop the skills to interpret and communicate statistical results effectively, gaining both theoretical understanding and hands-on experience in applying probabilistic and inferential methods.
